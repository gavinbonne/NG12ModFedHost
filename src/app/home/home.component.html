This page is part of the Host App and describes the border color definitions:

  • Blue is used to show the boundry of the Host App container
  • Orange is used to show the boundry of the Host App router-outlet
  • Red is used to show the boundry of the MFE Billing page
  • Purple is used to show the boundry of the MFE Health Tracker Widget
  • Teal is used to show the boundry of the MFE Article Tile
  • Green is used to show the boundry of the MFE Survey Tile
